Why MedLedger is Needed?

1. Fake Medical Reports

Manual and paper-based medical reports can be forged or altered, leading to serious health and legal issues.

2. No Verification System

Hospitals lack a centralized and trusted system to verify the originality of issued medical documents.

3. Data Tampering

Without cryptographic protection, medical data can be modified without detection.

4. MedLedger Solution

Blockchain ensures immutability while QR codes enable instant public verification of reports.

System Features

1

Only verified hospitals are allowed to upload medical reports.

2

Each report is converted into a cryptographic hash and stored securely on blockchain.

3

A QR code is generated and printed on the medical report.

4

Anyone can scan the QR and upload the report PDF for verification.
